# Seven Soundess Violations (7SV): Data

This folder contains *data* collected in experiments/tests/studies related to the 7SV.

- ```data-20260617.csv```: Data from a test (```/testsheets/prepared```) at Technical University of Munich, Campus Heilbronn.
    - ```group```: The group ("A" or "B").
	- ```uniqueId```: A unique identifier of each participant.
	- ```sepre01``` - ```sepre04```: A 7-Likert scale rated item about self assessment of the participants (values 1-7 and NA) in the pre-test.
	- ```sepost01``` - ```sepost05```: A 7-Likert scale rated item about self assessment of the participants (values 1-7 and NA) in the post-test.
	- ```mapre01``` - ```mapre05```: Marking exercises with values of 0-2 (points) and NA in the pre-test.
	- ```mapost01``` - ```mapost05```: Marking exercises with values of 0-2 (points) and NA in the post-test.
	- ```oapre01``` - ```oapre05```: Completing exercises with values of 0-2 (points) and NA in the pre-test.
	- ```oapost01``` - ```oapost05```: Completing exercises with values of 0-2 (points) and NA in the post-test.
